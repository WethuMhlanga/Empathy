About Clinic


It is part of a network of clinics providing high-quality, affordable and accessible care to South Africans. This network is an alternative to free public health facilities for millions of South Africans, thus improving collective community health, reducing loss of productivity due to health care seeking and illness, improving outcomes for children, and reducing the burden on overstretched state health care facilities. Clinics are owned and run by nurses who are empowered by owning their own businesses and creating agency and innovation in how they provide critical healthcare services.

Empathy is run by Promise Magagula, a medical professional and someone who cares about giving the best healthcare services for you and your family. Her goal is to bring better healthcare experiences and people's trust in the South African healthcare system. She aims to bring quality, efficient and most of all affordable healthcare for the community.
